Evolution of Internet

1964:Developed the ARPANET

1969:The first message was sent from one computer

1970: Vint Cerft invented a way to introduce computers across the globe to each other in a virtual space

1971: Ray Tomlinson was developing the first system to send mail back and forth between the users of ARPANET

1989: The internet then gives birth to the web.

1983: The TCP/IP became the standard way to communicate
